Questions

Honest answers.

Is Artha SEBI registered? ›
Artha is a financial analytics and intelligence platform. We provide data, analysis, and information — we do not give personalised buy or sell recommendations. We are on the path to SEBI Research Analyst registration.
How does Artha make money if it doesn't sell products? ›
Pure subscription revenue — ₹299/mo Premium, ₹699/mo Family. We earn zero commission from any mutual fund, broker, or financial product. Our only revenue is your fee. That's the alignment.
How is this different from INDmoney or Kuvera? ›
INDmoney and Kuvera may earn revenue through mutual fund distribution, trail commissions, or product referrals — a legitimate model. Artha's only revenue is your subscription fee. We distribute no financial product and earn trail on no fund. We also show you our math: every score has the raw value, the category context, and the confidence level — no hidden star ratings.
What can I actually do with the free tier? ›
Everything that matters for portfolio honesty: upload your CAS, see MF overlap analysis (the matrix + pairwise overlap %), get 7-dimension fund quality scores, view tax intelligence (LTCG harvest opportunities, IDCW flags, regime hint), and run scenarios in the tax simulator. The free tier is the real product — Premium adds daily AI nudges and the co-pilot chat.
Does Artha give buy/sell recommendations? ›
No. We compute math from your data — overlap percentages, fund quality scores, tax scenarios — and surface patterns. We never tell you to buy or sell anything. The decision is yours, and for tax matters we strongly recommend you verify with a qualified CA before acting on any scenario the simulator shows.
How accurate is the tax math? ›
All slab rates and exemptions are FY 2025-26 (Budget 2025) — LTCG 12.5%, STCG 20%, ₹1.25L exemption, new regime ₹4L basic + ₹60K rebate. But: a CAS gives us closing snapshots, not transaction history. So we can't know your actual purchase dates or YTD realised gains. We compute approximations and tell you what they're approximating. Always verify with your CA before acting.
Is my financial data safe? ›
CAS PDFs are parsed in your session and never stored. Account-level data, when you create an account in Premium, is encrypted at rest and in transit. We do not sell or share your data with any third party. Ever.

Our privacy commitment

Your data, honestly.

Artha is built on a simple idea: a financial product should align with you, not against you. That means we don't sell data, we don't run ads, and we don't sit between you and your money. This page tells you exactly what we do with the information you share.

Last updated: 7 June 2026
Effective: 7 June 2026
Governs: arthawealth.co
In one breath
  • We parse your CAS in memory and discard the PDF after computing your view.
  • We do not sell, share, or monetise your portfolio data — ever.
  • You can request deletion at any time, and we'll wipe everything within 7 days.
  • We comply with India's Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023 (DPDPA).
  • We run no third-party trackers or advertising scripts on our pages.

01 Who we are

Artha is a portfolio intelligence product operated independently. Our principal place of business is in Mumbai, Maharashtra, India. For privacy-related questions, contact our Grievance Officer (details at the bottom of this page).

Artha is not a SEBI-registered Investment Adviser, Portfolio Manager, or distributor of mutual funds. We do not provide personalised investment advice, and nothing on Artha should be construed as a recommendation to buy or sell any security.

02 What we collect

Different surfaces of the product handle different data. Here's exactly what each one touches:

When you visit our website — your browser sends standard technical information: IP address, browser version, page accessed, referring page. We use these only for security, error diagnosis, and rough usage counts. We do not use third-party analytics that profile you across sites.

When you join the waitlist — we collect your email address and, optionally, your name. We use this only to email you product updates and your early-access invitation. We never share this email with third parties.

When you upload a Consolidated Account Statement (CAS) — your CAS PDF contains mutual fund holdings (folio numbers, schemes, units, NAVs, values) and identifying information (your name, email, PAN, mobile number). The PDF is sent over an encrypted HTTPS connection to our server, where it is parsed in memory and discarded. We retain only the derived portfolio analysis (current holdings, valuation, allocation), not the raw PDF and not the embedded personal information beyond your masked PAN.

When you use the screener or Deep Dive — we log your query text temporarily to fulfil the request via our AI provider, and we cache anonymised results to save tokens. Nothing about your identity is sent to the AI provider.

03 What we don't do

Things Artha will never do
  • Sell, rent, or share your portfolio data with third parties for any commercial purpose.
  • Use your portfolio data to train AI models, including our own.
  • Receive commissions, kickbacks, or referral fees from mutual fund houses, brokers, or insurance distributors. Our subscription is our only revenue.
  • Display third-party advertisements on Artha pages.
  • Track you across websites with cookies, pixels, fingerprinting, or any other adtech mechanism.
  • Share data with the government except where compelled by lawful order, in which case we will notify you unless legally prohibited.

04 How we use your data

Each piece of data we collect serves one of these specific purposes — and nothing else:

  • Portfolio computation: parsing your CAS to show you your actual portfolio view, live NAV, allocation, and gain/loss.
  • Service operation: caching live NAVs and AMFI master data so the product is fast and doesn't hammer external APIs.
  • Communication: sending you portfolio insights, product updates, and (if you subscribe) billing receipts.
  • Security: detecting and preventing fraud, abuse, or unauthorised access.
  • Legal compliance: meeting our obligations under Indian financial and data protection law.

If we ever want to use your data for any new purpose, we'll ask first.

05 Where your data lives

Artha's backend runs on Railway (a cloud platform). Live NAV data comes from mfapi.in and AMFI India. AI-powered features call Anthropic's API. We use Cloudflare as our CDN.

All data transmission between you, our servers, and these services is encrypted in transit (HTTPS / TLS 1.2+). Our cache files contain only anonymised market data — no personal information.

When user accounts arrive (later in 2026), we'll store your account email and portfolio summary on Supabase, encrypted at rest, with row-level security so nobody else's queries can read your data.

06 Your rights under DPDPA

India's Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023 gives you specific rights over data we hold about you. Artha honours all of them:

Your rightWhat it meansHow to use it
Access See what data Artha holds about you. Email us; we'll respond in 14 days.
Correction Fix anything inaccurate or incomplete. Email us with the correction.
Erasure Delete all your data from our systems. Use the "Delete my data" option, or email us.
Withdraw consent Stop us from processing your data. Equivalent to erasure — same channel.
Grievance Complain if we mishandle your data. Contact the Grievance Officer below.
Nominate Designate someone to exercise your rights if you cannot. Email us with details.

All requests under these rights are free of charge and we respond within 14 days. If a request would require us to retain certain data for legal reasons (such as billing records), we'll explain what we can and can't delete.

07 Data retention

We hold your data only as long as it serves you, with these specific limits:

  • CAS PDFs: deleted from memory the moment parsing completes — typically within 10 seconds of upload. Never written to disk.
  • Parsed portfolio data: stored only in your browser's localStorage until accounts launch. When accounts arrive, you'll be able to set your own retention preference (default 12 months of activity).
  • Waitlist emails: retained until you launch your account or unsubscribe.
  • Server logs: retained for 30 days for security and debugging, then deleted automatically.
  • Aggregated and fully-anonymised metrics: may be retained indefinitely for product improvement (e.g. "average portfolio size is ₹X" with no link to individual users).

08 Cookies and similar technologies

Artha uses minimal first-party cookies — strictly for keeping you logged in (when accounts arrive) and remembering your settings. We do not use cookies for tracking, profiling, or advertising. No third-party tracking cookies are set on any Artha page.

We use localStorage in your browser to cache your last-parsed portfolio so a page refresh doesn't make you re-upload. This data stays on your device and is never sent to our servers. You can clear it via your browser's "Clear site data" option at any time.

09 Children

Artha is intended for adults (18+) managing their own finances. We do not knowingly collect data from anyone under 18. If you believe a child has shared data with us, contact the Grievance Officer and we'll delete it.

10 Security

We follow standard security practices: HTTPS everywhere, encrypted credentials at rest, principle-of-least-privilege access to internal systems, and routine security reviews. Despite this, no system is ever 100% secure. If we discover a breach affecting your data, we'll notify you and the Data Protection Board of India within 72 hours as DPDPA requires.

11 Changes to this policy

We may update this policy as the product evolves, the law changes, or we learn better ways to protect your data. When we make material changes, we'll email registered users at least 14 days before they take effect and update the "Last updated" date at the top.
